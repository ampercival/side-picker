How it Works

1. Setup Factions: Choose a preset game or enter faction names manually.

2. Add Players: Enter player names. Each player gets a card.

3. Rank Choices:
• Drag specific factions to Preferences (Green) for high points.
• Drag disliked factions to Banned (Red) to block them.
• Leave others in Available.

4. Optimize: Click 'Optimize & Assign'. The algorithm maximizes total group happiness score.

Pro-Tip: Use "Lock" on a player to exclude them from the Randomize button.
